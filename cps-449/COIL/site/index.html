Project Overview

Our team is building an AI-powered fraud detection system for banking transactions. The platform analyzes transaction behavior in real time to flag suspicious activity, reduce false positives, and help banks respond faster to potential fraud cases.

This project is being developed by students at the University of Dayton, in collaboration with university students from Vietnam, with a focus on practical deployment, scalable cloud architecture, and explainable model outputs so analysts can trust and validate each prediction.
